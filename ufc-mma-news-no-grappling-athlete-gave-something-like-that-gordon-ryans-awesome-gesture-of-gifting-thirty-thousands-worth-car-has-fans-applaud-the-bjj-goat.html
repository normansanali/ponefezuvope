    After undergoing a painful tonsillectomy surgery, Gordon Ryan is focusing on his recovery and spending some time away from competitive tournaments. During this long stretch of competitive absence, he considered this to be the best opportunity to connect with his fans. The BJJ ace recently announced a contest on his social media account as he declared to giveaway his Mazda Miata. The American submission grappler, who accumulated wealth and fame due to his BJJ exploits, wanted to make “things possible” for someone.

    The 5-time ADCC world champion updated the fans on his giveaway and expressed pleasure as he believed he added value to people’s life. Further, he admitted himself to be fortunate enough to be in a position to contribute to people. This selfless gesture of his evoked a sense of respect in fans for ‘The King’.

    Gordon Ryan fulfills his promise to gift his Mazda Miata

    The 27-year-old turned to his Instagram handle as he revealed his Miata has found a new owner, overcoming some hiccups.

    He penned, “Thank you all for participating in this, and I’m sorry it took so long! I’m very fortunate enough to be in a position where I can add a little bit of value to people’s lives, especially ones that support me”

    “Despite the hiccups, this car is finally gone and has a new owner. It’s one of the most fun cars I’ve ever driven, enjoy it, earl 🙂,” he added later.

    Similar to his noble gesture, the 2-time IBJJF No-Gi world champion is willing to go a step further and asked for responses from fans as to what he should give away next. Besides, he admitted he was willing to part away with his Chevrolet Corvette C6. Fans were quick to respond to this wholesome gesture with a healthy dose of humor and humility.
