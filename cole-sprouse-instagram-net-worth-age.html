    Cole Sprouse Bio: Age, Brother, Height

    The 26-years-of-age Italian actor was born as Cole Mitchell Sprouse on 4 August 1992 in Arezzo, Tuscany, Italy. He is the twin brother of an American actor and entrepreneur, Dylan Sprouse. Sprouse brothers were born when their American parents, Matthew Sprouse and Melanie Wright were teaching at an English language school in Tuscany. Four months after Cole’s birth, the family moved back to their parents’ native Long Beach, California.

    Standing tall at a height of 6 feet (1.83 m), Cole started attending New York University in 2011. Subsequently, he decided to enroll in the Gallatin School of Individualized Study, pursuing the humanities and in particular archaeology.

    Career, Net Worth

    Cole commenced his acting career starring as Patrick Kelly in the American sitcom, Grace Under Fire in 1993. He was with the show till its final season in 1998. He later made his big-screen debut as Julian McGrath in the 1999 comedy flick, Big Daddy.

    Cole Sprouse as Jughead Jones on Riverdale (Picture: IMDB)

    Cole’s other child appearance came as Ben Geller in one of the greatest sitcom ever, Friends, from 2000 to 2002. He later gained much attention as Cody Martin in the teen sitcom, The Suite Life of Zack & Cody (2005-2008), and its spin-off show The Suite Life on Deck (2008-2011). He is currently known for his main role of Jughead Jones in teen drama Riverdale (2017-present).

    The promising actor has appeared in movies like Diary of a Sex Addict (2001), Apple Jack (2003), Kung Fu Magoo (2010), and Five Feet Apart (2019). He bags the commanding net worth of $9 Million.

    Cole Sprouse Splits Up With His Girlfriend

    The news sources have claimed that Cole Sprouse and his actress girlfriend, Lili Reinhart have decided to part ways after nearly two years of dating. The Riverdale costars first bagged media attention in July 2017 after they were spotted making out at Comic-Con. However, they confirmed their romance in April 2018 after caught kissing in Paris.

    Cole Sprouse with ex-girlfriend Lili Reinhart (Picture: Stylecaster)

    As their relationship went south, the couple was spotted keeping distance from each other at the Entertainment Weekly Comic-Con party in San Diego on Saturday, 21 July 2019. Cole has formerly dated a number of girls including Bree Morgan, Kathleen Herles, and Maiara Walsh.
