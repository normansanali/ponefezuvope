    Dustin Andrews Obituary,  Death – Dustin Allen Andrews, a 29-year-old resident of Portland, sadly passed away on December 12, 2023, at Tristar Portland E.R. Born in Gallatin, TN, on December 21, 1993, to Steven Allen Andrews and the late Kami Dawn Gulley, Mr. Andrews leaves behind a legacy of cherished memories. Survived by his father, Steven Allen Andrews of Portland, TN, Mr. Andrews is also mourned by his sisters, Stephanie Andrews of Lafayette, TN, Keisha Hodges of Westmoreland, TN, and Jasmine Hodges of Portland, TN. His grandparents, Richard and Evelyn Brassell of Portland, TN, share in the grief of his untimely departure.

    A funeral service to celebrate the life of Mr. Andrews will take place at 2:00 pm on Sunday, December 17th, 2023, at Wilkinson & Wiseman Funeral Home, with Bro. Larry Emery officiating the ceremony. Prior to the service, friends and family are invited to pay their respects during the visitation hours on Saturday, December 16th, 2023, from 11:00 am until 9:00 pm, and on Sunday, December 17th, 2023, from 10:00 am until 2:00 pm at Wilkinson & Wiseman Funeral Home.

    The final resting place for Mr. Andrews will be in Old Brush Cemetery. Pallbearers, including Josh Cook, Phillip Wilson, Cory McClain, Kurtis Holmes, Cleveland Smith, and Richard Whittemore, will bear the weight of their friend’s departure, while Richard Brassell and Brandon Gulley will serve as honorary pallbearers. As friends and family gather to bid farewell to Dustin Allen Andrews, the community joins in mourning the loss of a beloved son, brother, and friend, remembering the warmth he brought into their lives. May he rest in peace.
