    Alyssa Arce Career

    Alyssa Arce’s career in modeling began when she was just 18 years old. She started by doing small modeling gigs and slowly made her way up the ladder. She gained recognition when she was featured in Playboy magazine in July 2013, and later in the same year, she became the Playboy Playmate of the Month for July 2013. This was a significant achievement in her career and helped her gain a lot of popularity.

    After her success with Playboy, she began working with various high-end fashion brands such as Esquire, FHM, and Sports Illustrated. She has also appeared in numerous music videos, including Robin Thicke’s “Blurred Lines” and Trey Songz’s “Smart Phones.” Arce has also walked the ramp for several designers and has been a part of many fashion shows.

    Apart from her successful modeling career, she has also worked in the acting industry. She made her acting debut in the comedy film “Date Night” in 2010, where she played a minor role. Later, she appeared in the independent film “The Ultimate Legacy” in 2015. In the same year, she also appeared in the comedy series “School Dance.”
