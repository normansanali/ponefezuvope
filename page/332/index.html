COLUMBUS, Ohio (WCMH) – Will mail be delivered on Labor Day? Residents expecting mail or packages on Monday will have to wait an extra day as deliveries will be put on pause for Labor Day. The United States Postal Service recognizes Labor Day on Sept. 4 as one of 11 federal holidays and is closed. There is no mail delivery, caller service, or Post Office Box service. All services resume on Tuesday.
