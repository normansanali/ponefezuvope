    Professional Life

    Professionally, Tyrone Crawley is an American YouTube star, producer, as well as an actor. Before starting YouTube, he had a career in sales for eight years.

    His self-titled YouTube channel ‘Tyrone Magnus‘ has gained more than 1.94 million subscribers to date.

    He initially gained fame as a social media personality through YouTube. He created his YouTube channel on March 17, 2012. Initially, he made general vlogs and reviews of WWE shows.

    He evolved into reacting to and reviewing trailers and later videos of all kinds such as Try Not To Laugh and Vine Compilations. In 2013, he started on a journey to attract one million subscribers by the end of the year.

    His YouTube subscribers continued to grow when he started uploading reaction videos of movie trailers as well as answering dating advice questions from viewers.

    Since 2014, his channel has become concentrated originally on reacting to videos sent in by viewers.

    However, he still performs videos answering viewers’ questions along with occasional unboxing, general vlogs, and gaming.

    He attained his goal of one million subscribers on April 13, 2017. Before then, he would frequently end his videos by saying “One million subscribers, woo!”

    In November 2014, his reaction video to the Star Wars: Episode VII – The Force Awakens teaser garnered a lot of attention after being shared on Reddit.

    The video gained upwards of one million views. After this video, he catches the attention of many viewers.
