    According to celebrity net worth, Tom Felton earns a net worth of $20 million. Tom Felton is an actor best known for his role as Draco Malfoy in the Harry Potter films.

    he began attending acting classes at the Suggestions Theater Company in London.

    His first professional acting gig came at the age of eight when he appeared in a British commercial for Milky Bar chocolates. 

    In 2001, he landed the role of Draco Malfoy in Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone.

    The Harry Potter films made him a global star, and he has since gone on to appear in a number of other films and television shows. 

    In recent years, he has also begun working as a photographer and musician. Felton is a talented actor with a diverse range of interests. He is someone who is sure to continue to be successful in whatever he chooses to do.

    Career – What Does The Future Hold For This Talented Actor?

    Felton started his acting career in commercials and guest starred on the television series The Bill. In 2001 he starred as Peagreen Clock in the film The Borrowers. From 2002 to 2003 Felton starred as Louis T. Leonowens in the mini-series Lorna Doone. 

    From 2009 to 2010 he starred as Ash Kensington on the TV series Discworld: The Color of Magic. Felton has also appeared in the films 1934, Tomorrow Never Dies, Heartbeat, Thunderpants, Goose on the Loose, Moondance Alexander, Rise of the Planet of the Apes, In Secret, Against the Sun, and Risparmio Della salute.

    In 2016 he portrayed James Gordon Jr. / The Joker on the television series Gotham and will Blake on the series Feed the Beast.

    Tom Felton won three Young Artist Awards from 2001 to 2003 for Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone, Harry Potter and the Chamber of Secrets, and Harry Potter and the Prisoner of Azkaban respectively. 

    He was nominated for several MTV Movie Awards from 2006 to 2012 for Best Villain for Harry Potter and Goblet of Fire, Order of Phoenix, Half-Blood Prince, Deathly Hallows Part 1, and Deathly Hallows Part 2. 

    In 2008 he won an MTV Movie Award for Best Fight for Order of Phoenix. He has also won a Teen Choice Award from 2005 to 2008for Harry Potter and two People’s Choice Awards from 2013 to 2014.

    Tom Felton Recently Opened Up About Drug Abuse, Mental Health & Rehab

    He also revealed that throughout his early 20s, he started to drink to escape the realities that life threw upon him.

    He started to spend a lot of time at dive bars in Los Angeles while keeping in mind that the troubles often started to spill in his professional career as well. 

    “Drinking becomes a habit at the best of times,” Felton writes in ‘Beyond the Wand:

    The Magic and Mayhem of Growing Up a Wizard’. “When you’re drinking to escape a situation, even more so. The habit spilled out of the bar and, from time to time, on to set.”

    “It came to the point where I would think nothing of having a drink while I was working,” Felton stated further “I’d turn up unprepared, not the professional I wanted to be.

    The alcohol, though, wasn’t the problem. It was a symptom. The problem was deeper,” Felton stated while speaking about mental health and the issues he faced. 

    He also stated how his girlfriend Jade Olivia, managers, and lawyer staged an intervention for him demanding that he needs to get

    help immediately as all of them were worried about his drinking problem, career, and behavior. “Everybody in the room had written me a letter,” he said.

    “I listened to Jade and the others as they told me how concerned they were about my behavior, about my drinking and my substance abuse. I was in no state to hear them.”
