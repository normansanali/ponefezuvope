    Michael Turchin Wikipedia.

    Despite being notable as both actor and a painter, Micheal Turchin has not been featured on Wikipedia.

    Micheal Turchin was born to Bobby and Stephaine Turchin. His father worked for the Turchin Companies in Miami Beach as a general contractor and developer.
