    How did Ben Miles start his Professional Career?

    Ben made his acting debut in 1989 as Barnardo in the Shakespeare Theatre production of “Hamlet” in Stratford-upon-Royal Avon. In 1993, he appeared in a production of ‘The Two Gentlemen of Verona’ by the Royal Shakespeare Company at London’s Barbican Theatre.
