    Killer Coworker Tubi Plot Synopsis

    The Killer Coworker Tubi movie starts in the renowned wellness spa Serenity Gardens. Ivy is the CEO of the company and wanted to change its rules and system.

    Ivy and her best friend Zoe have been working there since the beginning of the company. The CEO made sure her employee worked hard every day.

    She even assigned work to each and every individual at the wellness spa. After Stella joins the company, various unusual events start to occur.

    At first, Stella tries to avoid it and is also involved in a relationship with the Kilman without knowing his true identity.

    Most of the staff of the Wellness Spa used to envy her for getting one of the reputed positions. She was an MBA graduate, smart and confident at her work.

    Her boyfriend Kilman was a womanizer and dated almost every girl in the spa. His character was different than the rest of the cast.

    Kilman is always onto something and involves himself in various crimes, illegal works, and deception as his side job.

    In the movie, there are a few superstitious things that are practiced like tarot card reading, tantric sessions as well as seeing Chakras.

    All of the characters are ready to kill each other in order to get what they want, there are so many interesting things that will keep viewers hooked till the end.

    After facing and seeing everything inside the wellness spa, Stella wanted to quit her job and stay out of the chaotic and spooky situation.

    But Ivy has something else planned for her so she promoted her and sent her boyfriend to London to start the new spa.

    There are some murder mysteries in the movie where Stella was portrayed as one of the potential murderers but hopefully, there are detectives who investigate the crime.
