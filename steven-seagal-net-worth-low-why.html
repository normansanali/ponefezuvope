    How Much Is Steven Seagal Worth Today?

    Net Worth: $14 million

    Given his huge list of accolades, especially in the TV space, most would assume that Steven Seagal would have a surging net worth these days, in the category of $100 million and above. However, this could not be further from the truth, as the actor is currently worth $14 million.

    In truth, given his reputation, we don't see this number climbing much higher and, given some controversies he was involved in, it might shrink even further. This is especially notable, as Steven Seagal was once making millions for roles, including $6.5 million for both Exit Wounds and Ticker.

    Ultimately, what really hurt Seagal's career was his reputation behind the scenes. Steven was not well-liked by his peers, and this started his ultimate downfall.

    Why Did Steven Seagal's Net Worth Plummet In Recent Years?

    Steven Seagal stopped making films in 2019 and entered into some poor business deals

    Steven Seagal has made some questionable decisions throughout his career. That includes entering businesses outside of acting, like his energy drink Steven Seagal's Lightning Bolt in 2005, which would eventually be discontinued. Steven Seagal claimed that he backpacked Asia to find the best ingredients for his energy drink, making mention that the drink offered an "untold power". He also endorsed failed products like a knife range along with aftershave creams.

    In 2020, Seagal lost more money thanks to his promotion of Bitcoiin2Gen. The actor kept vital information a secret, including his large pay to endorse the product, which was valued at a cool million. Once this came out, he was forced to fork over six figures in penalties.

    Ultimately, his biggest downfall was due to acting. Getting employed became a tough task, not only because actors spoke out against his behavior, but also because women such as Regina Simons, Julianna Margulies, and Portia De Rossi took to the public and discussed stories of abuse from the actor.

    What Is Steven Seagal Doing These Days?

    Steven Seagal is living in Russia where he has worked as a "special representative" to the US and Japan

    He's also in the headlines for the current war between Ukraine and Russia. Seagal is known for his relationship with Vladimir Putin and his work to getting a Russian citizenship. Steven did issue a statement on the matter, giving a very diplomatic answer.

    "Most of us have friends and family in Russia & Ukraine," the action star told Fox News Digital. "I look at both as one family and really believe it is an outside entity spending huge sums of money on propaganda to provoke the two countries to be at odds with each other. My prayers are that both countries will come to a positive, peaceful resolution where we can live & thrive together in peace."
