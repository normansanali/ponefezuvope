    Catherine Hickland Wiki

    Catherine Hickland was born in Fort Lauderdale, Florida, on February 11th, 1956. Her ethnicity is unknown, however, she is an American citizen.

    Mary K. Hickland, a dental ceramist, is her mother, and Arthur W. Hickland, a dentist, is her father. Her names are unknown to her two siblings, a brother, and a sister.

    Hickland attended and graduated from Fort Lauderdale High School in 1974. She later enrolled at Florida Atlantic University but dropped out after two years. She dropped out of university to work for National Airlines as a flight attendant.

    She was chosen for an airline campaign just after six months of training. She became interested in performing and left her airline job to pursue it. She relocated to Los Angeles to pursue her acting career.

    Career

    Catherine began her acting career in 1978 by participating in an episode of the television series Vega$. She appeared in The Seeding of Sarah Burns and Eight Is Enough in 1979. Texas, CHiPs, Airwolf, Knight Rider, Capitol, The Bold and the Beautiful, Werewolf, The Last Married Couple in America, To Race the Wind, Robowar – Robot da Guerra, Ghost Town, Witchery, and Taxi Killer are among the films and television shows in which she appeared in the 1980s.

    In the 1990s, she starred in the films Millions and Sweet Justice, as well as the television shows Loving and The City and One Life to Live. She starred in the television series Law & Order and Law & Order: Criminal Intent between 2001 and 2005. In 2007, she portrayed Millie Fresno in the film Mattie Fresno and the Holoflux Universe.
