    The former MLB player Felipe Vazquez is sentenced to prison for sexual assault. Does he have a wife? 

    Felipe Vazquez is a professional baseball pitcher from Venezuelan who has played for the Pittsburgh Pirates, Washington Nationals, and Tampa Bay Rays of the Major League Baseball (MLB). Vazquez was an All-Star in both 2018 and 2019.

    He is a convicted sex offender, placed on the restricted list by Major League Baseball and the Pittsburgh Pirates. Vazquez got arrested on September 17, 2019, on multiple charges of child pornography, sexual relationship with a 13-year-old female, and providing obscene materials to minors.

    Does Felipe Vazquez Have A Wife?

    Felipe Vazquez does not have a wife as he is not yet married. Vazquez rarely talked about the details of his dating life and relationship with the media. 

    Talking about his family, Felipe Vazquez has a sister, Prescilla Vazquez. She is a sports agent who mainly deals with football and baseball athletes. Prescilla started working as a realtor after moving to the USA. 

    Felipe did change his name from Rivero to Vazquez. He changed his last name in 2018 to match the surname of his sister as she had been a massive part of his career, reports USA Today.

    Felipe Vazquez’s estimated career earning is around $10,002,130, reports Spotrac. Vazquez signed a $22.5 million four-year contract deal with the Pittsburgh Pirates before the 2018 season. Felipe’s net worth details are currently private.

    Felipe Vazquez’s accuser is a 13-year-old girl who is originally from Lee County, Florida.

    Vazquez got arrested in September 2019 and confessed to the Pennsylvania police that he had sex with the girl in 2017, but not really, reports The New York Post.
