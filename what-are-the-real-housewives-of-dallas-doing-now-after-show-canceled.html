    What Are The Real Housewives Of Dallas Doing Now That The Show Has Ended?

    The Real Housewives Of Dallas series was not picked up for a sixth season in 2021. Fans of the show were disappointed but not shocked, as the program had been the lowest-rated housewives franchise for years.

    12 Jul 2024, 00:00

    The Real Housewives Of Dallas series was not picked up for a sixth season in 2021. Fans of the show were disappointed but not shocked, as the program had been the lowest-rated housewives franchise for years.

    RELATED: 'Real Housewives Of Dallas' Which Castmate Is The Oldest?

    The show saw plenty of pushback throughout its five seasons. Numerous accusations of racist behavior between castmates drew significant divides among both the women and the fan base. By the end of season 5, Dr. Tiffany Moon was refusing to shoot scenes with Kameron Westcott. Rather than recast, production decided to stop shooting the series altogether. While some fans are hopeful to see the Real Housewives of Dallas on their screens again soon, let's look at what the women have been up to since the cameras stopped rolling.

    7 Did Kary Brittingham Divorce Her Husband After The Real Housewives Of Dallas Was Canceled?

    The status of Kary Brittingham's relationship with her husband Eduardo was up-in-the-air for the entirety of season five. Kary was afraid to admit she was unhappy in her relationship. What she was even more fearful of was the financial insecurity that awaited her after divorcing her husband. She can be seen drinking excessively throughout the show's last season, with sources telling Us Weekly that's how the star began coping with the demise of the relationship. Fans felt her drinking brought out a dark side of her.

    RELATED: 'Real Housewives Of Dallas' What Is Cast Member Kary Brittingham's Net Worth?

    Kary announced the couple's divorce on Instagram hours before the airing of season five's lackluster reunion. The reality star has been divorced for almost a year now and, from the looks of her Instagram page, is having fun with her single life. She's working for a real estate firm in Texas while continuing to pursue her jewelry business. She also still hangs out with costar Kameron Westcott.

    6 Are Brandi Redmond And Stephanie Hollman Still Best Friends?

    The Real Housewives of Dallas fans were enthralled by Brandi and Stephanie's relationship from the get-go. Their hilarious antics and uninhibited ability to let loose made for A+ reality television.

    The girls had been friends long before the show aired, and despite a few gnarly fights during their time on the show, the two remain close friends to this day. They have a podcast together, Weekly Dose of BS, where they discuss what's going on in their lives.

    5 What Is Cary Dueber Doing After Leaving The Real Housewives Of Dallas?

    Cary made a few appearances on The Real Housewives of Dallas after electively moving to a friend of role in season four. She and her husband, Dr. Mark Dueber, are still a dynamite duo, taking the Dallas plastic surgery and injection scene by storm. They even expanded their practice Lemmon Avenue to Atlanta.

    According to her Instagram, Cary remains friends with many of her old costars, like Kameron Wescott, Leanne Locken, and Kary Brittingham.

    4 What Is D'Andra Simmons Doing Now That Real Housewives Of Dallas Is Over?

    D'andra and her mother, Momma Dee, were instant fan favorites. The mother and daughter's hilarious banter and honesty about the problems in their relationship spoke to viewers. Some fans were convinced the two would be back on Bravo in some capacity after the news of the cancelations, but there has yet to be a chance.

    The two were spotted at Bravocon 2022. They happily met with fans and strolled through the Bravoland Museum, which housed numerous iconic items from Bravo shows. They even took time to fix Momma Dee's wig display on loan from the matriarch for the event.

    RELATED: 'Real Housewives Of Dallas': Who Was D'Andra Simmons Before The Show?

    D'andra remains happily married to her husband, Jeremy Locke. She also added a new puppy to her family in 2021, Wickett Winslow II. According to her Instagram, she still hangs out with cast member Dr. Tiffany Mood.

    3 How Has Dr Tiffany Moons Life Changed Since Real Housewives Of Dallas?

    According to Dr. Tiffany Moon, she had conversations with producers after the season about the show's future. While she doesn't take credit for ensuring the cancelation, she said it was fascinating that it happened so soon after her conversations with staff members.

    Dr. Tiffany Moon has made a name for herself on social media since RHOD ended. She posts funny reels on Instagram weekly and engages with her fans. According to her Instagram, she's still working as an anesthesiologist, along with running her own candle line and being a full-time mom to twins. As for her castmates, it doesn't seem she has relationships with any of them besides longtime friend D'andra Simmons.

    2 What Has Leanne Locken Been Doing Since Leaving The Show?

    Leanne Locken famously told page six that The Real Housewives of Dallas would have never been canceled if she was still present. While the truth to that statement can't be measured, Leanne was a fan-favorite, despite how polarizing of a figure she could be.

    Since leaving the show, Leanne has focused on her career outside reality tv. She began acting again and was a featured model in numerous high-level publications, such as ReStyled Magazine. In an interview with DailyMailUK, the star said she didn't realize how much she needed to leave the show until she did. She compared it to being in an abusive relationship, noting she didn't know how happy she could be without it until the show was gone.

    Leanne still lives in Dallas with her husband, Rich Emberlin. She remains highly active in the Dallas charity scene and is friends with numerous old costars, such as Kameron Wescott and Cary Dueber.

    1 What Does Kameron Westscotts Life Look Like After Real Housewives?

    Kameron's husband surprised her with her dream house during the last season of The Real Housewives of Dallas. The couple and their children moved into the home shortly after filming wrapped, and Kameron seems to be in heaven. She and her home are featured on D Home Magazine's 2022 Holiday Issue cover.

    Kameron was facing a lot of heat during the last season of Dallas. Fans accused her of being racist to cast member Tiffany Moon. Because of the allegations of racism, Kameron hired a diversity coach to work with after the show aired. During an appearance on the podcast No Filter With Zack Peter, she promised to take accountability for any wrongdoings or sayings. She explained to Zack that she felt horrible about how she acted and spoke during the reunion. She took the time to apologize profusely to both Tiffany and the viewers.
