    Olympic: Who Are Chloe Kim Parents Jong Jin And Boran Yun Kim? 

    The Youth Olympic champion, Chloe Kim’s parents Jong Jin Kim, and Boran Yun Kim are among her biggest fans and supporters.

    Her father and mother emigrated from South Korea to have a decent lifestyle as per International Olympic Committee.

    Jong Jin, her father arrived in the US with $800 money and yet acquired a college degree in manufacturing engineering technology in 1982.

    Jong and Boran met in Switzerland while both of them were working as travel agents. They soon moved to Southern California where they raised their three daughters: Chloe and her elder sisters Tracy and Erica.

    He gave up his job as an engineer to train and travel with her full time to fulfill her destiny.
