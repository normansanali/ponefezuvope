    Whether she’s rocking the stage or our Instagram feeds, Jennifer Lopez always knows how to effortlessly add a little bit of spice to an otherwise ordinary look. Case in point? On Monday, the multi-hyphenate shared a roundup of stunning OOTD snaps that featured more than a few head-turning details — including what may just have been her tallest heels yet.

    In the photo dump, which Lopez captioned only with a sun emoji, the singer flexed her posing chops in front of a scenic outdoor fountain while dressed in gold and white from head to toe. Upping the drama of an average shirt dress, J.Lo slipped into a knee-length white button-up frock from Schiaparelli complete with gigantic slouchy puff sleeves and mismatched gold buttons. The A-lister continued with the gold theme by adding a holographic gold Cult Gaia clutch, dazzling gold drop earrings, and a pair of gold-framed sunglasses to accessorize her look, but the most jaw-dropping detail had to be her choice of footwear: towering gold platform heels that easily added at least six inches to her stature.

    As for glam, Jennifer pulled her long caramel hair back into a sleek low bun with a middle part and added a bright fuchsia lip to complement her typical bronzy makeup look.

    Lopez’s post came just hours after she detailed yet another seasonally confusing look when posting about an appearance at the fifth Daytime Beauty Awards. 

    In a separate post, the actress gave her 252 million Instagram followers an up-close look at the pink-and-white floral dress she wore to the ceremony, which featured a plunging V-neckline, a waist-cinching design, and a high-low hem.
