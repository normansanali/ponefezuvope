    Nigerian comedian and former music artist Tunde Olaoluwa Adekunle, popularly known as Tunde Ednut has urged his fans to do everything possible to make popular female housemate Liquorose leave the house.

    Tunde Ednut is one of the supporters of male housemate Whitemoney. Whitemoney is one of the housemates who have a lot of fans outside the house. Tunde Ednut has taken to his page to say that female housemate Liquorose must leave the BBNaija house in order to make way for Whitemoney to win because she is a threat to Whitemoney.
