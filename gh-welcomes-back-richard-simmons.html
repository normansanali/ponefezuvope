    GH Welcomes Back Richard Simmons

    Updated on July 18: Richard Simmons is returning to GH! Richard Simmons took to Twitter this afternoon to make an announcement that he will be making another guest appearance on General Hospital. Stay tuned. We will post if more details about his appearance emerge.

    16 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Updated on July 18:

    Richard Simmons is returning to “GH!” Richard Simmons took to Twitter this afternoon to make an announcement that he will be making another guest appearance on “General Hospital.” Stay tuned. We will post if more details about his appearance emerge.

    Off to do General Hospital this morning…………what fun!

    — Richard Simmons (@TheWeightSaint) July 18, 2013

    Previously Reported on March 4:

    Special appearance.

    Famed fitness fanatic Richard Simmons is headed back to Port Charles for a visit. The weight loss magnate played himself on the soap from 1979 until 1983. He will be returning for an appearance at the Nurses’ Ball. Look for him to pop up in two episodes. No word yet on the dates when you can see if he’s still sporting his trademark striped shorts.
