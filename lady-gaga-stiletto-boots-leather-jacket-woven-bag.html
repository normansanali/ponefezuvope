    Lady Gaga Looked Fabulously Cool in Stiletto Boots and a Leather Jacket on a Pizza Run

    29 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Has anyone else ever looked half as cool as Lady Gaga casually picking up pizza? Probably not.

    The multi-hyphenate was recently spotted on a pizza run in Malibu, accompanied by her longtime boyfriend, philanthropic entrepreneur Michael Polansky.

    For the rare joint outing, Gaga cut a striking figure in a sophisticated ensemble composed of a buttery dark brown leather blazer, black Alo leggings, and glossy black croc-embossed knee-high boots with stiletto heels. She coordinated the look with her accessories, adding a black leather woven tote bag and a pair of black shades. She kept her glam to a minimum, simply tying off her signature platinum blonde hair into a messy low bun.

    Meanwhile, Polansky wore a laid-back outfit, opting for an orange puffer with green and gray stripes down the middle, olive green pants, a black baseball cap, and black-and-white sneakers.

    BACKGRID

    The couple, who went public with their relationship back in 2020, appear to be going strong despite recent reports claiming they’d broken up.

    A source dished on the nature of Gaga and Polansky’s loving and supportive relationship in a 2021 interview with Entertainment Tonight.

    “Michael is her North Star. He is such a grounding and guiding presence for her They are the real deal. He loves Lady Gaga, but he’s in love with Stefani,” the insider said. The “Shallow” singer’s real name is Stefani Germanotta.

    The source said that prefers to keep his personal affairs as private as possible, adding, “He loves the theatrics and her artistry, but he is very happy being out of the spotlight and letting her shine.”
