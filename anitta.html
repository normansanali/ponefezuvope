    Anitta in 2018, photo by Prphotos

    Birth Name: Larissa de Macedo Machado

    Place of Birth: Rio de Janeiro, Brazil

    Date of Birth: 30 March, 1993

    Ethnicity: Brazilian [African, Portuguese, possibly other]

    Anitta is a Brazilian singer, songwriter, television host, actress, and businessperson. She is the daughter of Miriam Tereza de Macedo and Mauro Machado.

    A picture of Anitta’s father can be seen here.

    Anitta has said:

    My father and his family are black… and my mother is white. I just do not have time to go to the beach. When I go, you’ll see…

    Anitta’s paternal grandfather was Luiz Machado de Azeredo/Azevedo (the son of Raimundo Machado de Azeredo and Hermenegilda/Ermenegilda de Avelar). Luiz was born in Minas Gerais. Raimundo was a noted craftsman who designed the Pipiripau Nativity Scene, a major cultural asset from Minas Gerais. He was the son of Gabriel Machado de Azeredo and Maria Luiza. Hermenegilda was the daughter of Pedro Cassimiro Gonçalves and Maria Gonçalves Vieira.

    Anitta’s paternal grandmother is named Stella/Stela/Estella Matutina.

    Anitta’s maternal grandfather was named Pedro J. Macedo. Pedro was from Paraíba.

    Anitta’s maternal grandmother was named Maria Glorete/Gloriete. Maria was from Paraíba.

    Source: Death record of Anitta’s paternal grandfather, Luiz Machado de Azeredo/Azevedo – https://www.familysearch.org
